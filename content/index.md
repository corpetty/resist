# Resistance Theory Project

A comprehensive framework for analyzing resistance movements, understanding their dynamics, and developing strategic thinking about social change.

This project explores a fundamental tension: **non-violent resistance requires solving difficult collective action problems, while violence offers accessible individual agency but usually fails strategically.**
